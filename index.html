Who I am

My name is Jordan Roberson. I am currently a student at Practicum by Yandex, learning to become a full stack web developer

I currently work as an Autonomous Nurse Practitioner in my own clinic. What does that mean? I am able to work INDEPENDENTLY without a physician over me. I have been a nurse for almost 10 years and worked as a nurse practitioner for almost 5.

I first became interested in web development when I was around 10 years old...messing around with livejournal and neopets. I fell in love with the ability to alter things exactly how I wanted them. I was originally a computer science major however was told that since I am a woman that I would never succeed. Being a young and impressionable 18 year old I decided to then change my major to nursing. I loved nursing however I've always been beyond passionate with technology. With the COVID rise I feel that healthcare has gone in more of a business direction versus a patient centered model. Thus resulting in my decision to pursue tech specifically web development.
